Postdoctoral Researcher | Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL)

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the School of Computer and Communication Sciences of EPFL and a member of the Data Intensive Applications and Systems group, where I work with Anastasia Ailamaki. My re­search in­ter­ests lie in the areas of databases and real-time analytics, and specifically in Approximate Query Processing and Stream Processing.

I completed my PhD in the Computing Systems group of NTUA advised by Nectarios Koziris and Dimitrios Tsoumakos. While doing my PhD, I spent some time in the U.S. where I worked in IBM as a research intern. Be­fore that, I stud­ied Elec­tri­cal and Com­puter En­gi­neer­ing at NTUA.
